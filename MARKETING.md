## 🎬 Demo Trailer Storyboard: "The 50-Turn Empire"
**Target Length:** 45–60 Seconds  
**Audio Style:** Starts atmospheric (Medieval Lute/Cello), ramps up into rhythmic war drums.

### Sequence:
1. **[0:00-0:05] The Hook (The Empty Board)**
   - *Visual:* Close-up on the starting Capital hex surrounded by dark Fog of War.
   - *Action:* A Scout moves forward; fog peels back to reveal a stunning snowy **Winter Biome**.
   - *Overlay:* "ONE WORLD."

2. **[0:05-0:15] The Expansion (Satisfying Pops)**
   - *Visual:* Fast montage of building placements and glowing golden borders pushing outward.
   - *Action:* Placing Lumberjacks/Farms. Player clicks "END TURN."
   - *Highlight:* Cinematic camera pan showing **+15 🌾** and **+10 🪵** floating from buildings.
   - *Overlay:* "FIFTY TURNS."

3. **[0:15-0:30] The Conflict (Tactics & Danger)**
   - *Visual:* HUD switches from Blue (Economy) to Red (Military).
   - *Action:* A Goblin Camp appears in the Wasteland. Knight/Archer deployed.
   - *Highlight:* Archer fires a golden arrow in a smooth arc, hitting a Goblin (-4 HP + shake).
   - *Overlay:* "SURVIVE THE HORDE."

4. **[0:30-0:40] The Climax (Cinematic Chaos)**
   - *Visual:* Rapid cuts: "Year of Dust" event modal, Tech Tree unlocking 'Ballistics', dense City Districts.
   - *Action:* Towers firing cyan bolts at advancing Goblins.
   - *Audio:* Music reaches peak intensity.

5. **[0:40-0:45] The Meta-Loop (Roguelite)**
   - *Visual:* Fade to the glass-morphism **Eternal Palace**.
   - *Action:* Buying "Royal Scout" upgrade, clicking "BEGIN NEW CHRONICLE."
   - *Overlay:* "BUILD YOUR LEGACY."

6. **[0:45-0:50] Outro**
   - *Visual:* Logo fades in over a sweeping, zoomed-out shot of a massive kingdom.
   - *Text:* "Play the Beta Now in your Browser."

### Short Description (itch.io / Steam Sidebar)
> *Kingdom of Hex* is a fast-paced, turn-based roguelite strategy game. Explore procedural hex worlds, manage a tight economy with adjacency bonuses, and defend your borders from encroaching enemies. Every 50-turn run earns you Legacy Points to unlock permanent meta-upgrades.

### Long Description (Store Page / README)
**A Strategy Game Respectful of Your Time**  
Welcome to *Kingdom of Hex*, where deep 4X strategy meets the addictive loop of a roguelite. You don't need 10 hours to finish a campaign—you have exactly 50 turns to amass 1000 Gold. Every decision matters, every tile counts, and every run builds your permanent legacy.

**Key Features:**
- 🗺️ **Procedural Hex Worlds:** Push back the fog of war to discover diverse biomes. Harvest in the Temperate plains, mine in the Winter tundra, or hunt goblins for double bounties in the Ashen Wastelands.
- 🏘️ **City-Building Puzzle:** Optimization is key. Group Farms for bonuses, and surround Markets with buildings to multiply gold. Manage Food carefully: a starving army is a dead army.
- ⚔️ **Tactical Combat:** Recruit Scouts, Archers, and Knights. Use A* pathfinding to outmaneuver the Goblin hordes, and build Towers to automate your defense.
- 🧪 **Tech Tree & World Events:** Build Libraries to generate Science and unlock advanced warfare. Face randomized World Events every 10 turns that force difficult strategic sacrifices.
- 🏛️ **The Eternal Palace:** Win or lose, every run converts to Legacy Points. Return to the Palace to buy permanent starting bonuses for your next Chronicle.

## 🚀 Beta Launch Tips
1. **GIFs are King:** Record a 5-second high-quality GIF of the **"End Turn" sequence**. It is the most visually satisfying part of the game and perfect for r/WebGames or r/IndieGaming.
2. **Emphasize WebGPU:** Developers and tech enthusiasts love WebGPU right now. Tagging Three.js and WebGPU communities will drive high-quality initial traffic.
3. **Seeded Sharing:** Encourage players to post their **Seeds** alongside screenshots of their "High Score" kingdoms to build community engagement.
